FAQ

Executive Advisory FAQ

How does candidate anonymization work?

When you anonymize your profile, our internal system replaces your name, employer name, and specific education metadata with generic descriptions (e.g. "CFO at Swiss Biotech"). Employers can view your skills but must submit a request to reveal your identity, which you must manually approve.

What are your placement fees?

We work strictly on a retained basis. We charge a set advisory fee calculated as a percentage of the placement's first-year target compensation. This fee is paid in milestones throughout the execution blueprint, guaranteeing our full loyalty to the mandate.

Do you manage interim placements?

Yes. We have a pre-screened directory of operational executives who can step into C-Suite vacancies, post-merger integration, or turnaround events within 72 hours.
